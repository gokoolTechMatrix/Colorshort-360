============================================================
1. HOW DOES A CUSTOMER REQUEST A SERVICE?
============================================================

Customer can raise service through:
+ After Sales Order (Auto Service Reminder)
+ Phone Call to Service Desk
+ WhatsApp / Website Service Form
+ Walk-in Service Request
+ Warranty / AMC Claim
+ Emergency Breakdown Call

==> All these become:
SERVICE LEADS / SERVICE TICKETS


============================================================
2. WHO CREATES THE SERVICE LEAD?
============================================================

+ ONLY SERVICE COORDINATOR (OFFICE ROLE)

Service Coordinator creates tickets for:
+ Product Installation
+ Breakdown Complaint
+ Free Service
+ Paid Service
+ AMC Service
+ Warranty Service

VISIBILITY AFTER CREATION:
+ Service Coordinator
+ Service Manager
+ Assigned Service Executive (After Assignment)

NOTE:
+ Service Executive (Field Engineer) CANNOT create service leads.


============================================================
3. WHAT DATA IS STORED IN A SERVICE LEAD?
============================================================

+ Customer Name
+ Mobile Number
+ Address / GPS Location
+ Product Name & Model
+ Serial Number
+ Warranty / AMC Status
+ Complaint Type
+ Detailed Problem Description
+ Priority:
  - Normal
  - High
  - Emergency
+ Preferred Visit Date & Time
+ Service Type:
  - Free
  - Paid
  - AMC
  - Installation


============================================================
4. WHO ASSIGNS THE SERVICE EXECUTIVE (FIELD ENGINEER)?
============================================================

+ ONLY SERVICE MANAGER HAS FINAL ASSIGNMENT AUTHORITY

ASSIGNMENT FLOW:

Service Coordinator
        |
        v
Creates Ticket
        |
        v
SERVICE MANAGER (Assigns Engineer)
        |
        v
Service Executive (Field Engineer)

ASSIGNMENT BASIS:
+ Location
+ Workload
+ Skill Level
+ Emergency Priority

Once Assigned:
+ Mobile/App Notification sent to Engineer
+ Job Card Auto-Created


============================================================
5. WHAT DOES THE SERVICE EXECUTIVE (FIELD ENGINEER) DO?
============================================================

Service Executive CAN:
+ View Assigned Jobs Only
+ Call the Customer
+ Navigate to Customer Location
+ Inspect & Diagnose Problem
+ Update Job Status:
  - On the Way
  - Work Started
  - Spare Required
  - Work On Hold
  - Work Completed
+ Upload:
  - Before / After Photos
  - Customer Signature
  - Work Completion Report

Service Executive CANNOT:
+ Change Service Price
+ Approve Warranty
+ Approve Spare Cost
+ Close Ticket Finally (Only Request Closure)


============================================================
6. WHO HANDLES SPARES, PAYMENT & APPROVALS?
============================================================

+ FINAL AUTHORITY = SERVICE MANAGER

APPROVALS INCLUDE:
+ Paid Service Charges
+ Spare Part Cost
+ Warranty Claim
+ Repeat Visit
+ Final Job Closure

APPROVAL FLOW:

Service Executive (Request)
        |
        v
Service Coordinator (Verifies)
        |
        v
SERVICE MANAGER (Approves / Rejects)
        |
        v
Status Updated


============================================================
7. WHAT DOES THE SERVICE COORDINATOR DO DAILY?
============================================================

Service Coordinator is the CONTROL DESK:

+ Create Service Tickets
+ Edit Service Tickets
+ Assign Priority
+ Follow-up with Customers
+ Track Engineer Live Status
+ Track Pending Jobs
+ Coordinate with Spares / Inventory
+ Request Approvals from Manager
+ Send Invoices to Customer
+ Close Ticket after Manager Approval
+ Handle Customer Feedback


============================================================
8. WHAT DOES THE SERVICE MANAGER CONTROL?
============================================================

Service Manager has FULL CONTROL:

+ View ALL Service Tickets
+ Assign / Reassign Engineers
+ Approve:
  - Service Charges
  - Spare Costs
  - Warranty & AMC
  - Job Closures
+ View SLA & Delay Reports
+ Monitor Engineer Performance
+ Handle Escalations
+ Emergency Job Reassignment


============================================================
9. HOW IS THE CUSTOMER INFORMED?
============================================================

Customer receives automatic alerts via:
+ SMS
+ WhatsApp
+ App Notification

ALERTS SENT FOR:
+ Service Request Received
+ Engineer Assigned
+ Engineer On the Way
+ Work Completed
+ Invoice Generated
+ Feedback Request


============================================================
10. COMPLETE SERVICE WORKFLOW (SIMPLE)
============================================================

1. Customer raises service request
2. Service Coordinator creates service ticket
3. Service Manager assigns Service Executive
4. Service Executive visits customer
5. Engineer uploads work report
6. Service Coordinator verifies & follows up
7. Service Manager approves charges / warranty
8. Invoice generated
9. Customer notified
10. Service ticket closed


============================================================
11. FINAL RESPONSIBILITY SUMMARY
============================================================

SERVICE COORDINATOR (OFFICE ROLE):
+ Create & manage service tickets
+ Communicate with customers
+ Track engineers
+ Request approvals
+ Share invoices & reports

SERVICE EXECUTIVE (FIELD ENGINEER):
+ Visit customer
+ Diagnose & repair
+ Upload job proof
+ Request spare & approvals
+ NO financial authority

SERVICE MANAGER:
+ Assign & control engineers
+ Approve service cost & warranty
+ Monitor full service operation
